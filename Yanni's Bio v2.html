Yanni's Bio

A short personal history

Born in Ohio, where I've lived most of my life. Lived in Greece for nine years when I was young. Studied Aerospace at Embry-Riddle
but transferred to Kent State, where I received my B.S. in Aerospace Engineering Technology. I worked for a few years at Goodyear and
Bridgestone/Firestone doing Mold Design using CAD. Then I went to Boston to study Theology. I received my M.Div. from Holy Cross Greek
Orthodox School of Theology. I was ordained as a priest in 2005 and have been serving the Greek Orthodox Church in America for 13 years.
I have served in four different church communities. I'm taking a little break from the ministry to pursue coding. I am married, have
five children and love playing soccer and reading. I also like video games.
